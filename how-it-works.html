FAQs

Common Questions

Is Event Vista free for customers?

Yes! Customers can browse, compare, and contact vendors completely free. We also offer a premium membership with additional benefits.

How are vendors verified?

We verify vendors through business registration checks, portfolio reviews, and collect genuine reviews from past customers. Only verified vendors can list on our platform.

Do you charge commission on bookings?

No! We don't take any commission from customers or vendors on bookings. Our revenue comes from vendor membership fees and premium features.

Which cities do you cover?

We currently operate in 50+ cities across India including Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Chennai, Hyderabad, Pune, and many tier 2 cities. We're expanding rapidly!

How do I become a vendor?

Simply click "Join as Vendor" and complete the registration process. Once verified, you can create your profile and start receiving leads.
