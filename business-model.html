Questions

Pricing FAQs

Can I try before I buy?

Absolutely! Customers can use our Free plan forever. Vendors get a 14-day free trial of Professional features before deciding.

Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?

Yes, you can change your plan at any time. Upgrades are effective immediately, and downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and popular wallets like Paytm and PhonePe.

Is there a refund policy?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied, contact us for a full refund.
